What's Up, SA

A full-stack web application that is focused on striving to connect communities in San Antonio, Texas by compiling public city-based events and functions. Users are able to submit events, add friends, and show interest in other events. In addition, users can upload images to their profile and events and their interests will be shared with their friends list. Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, Spring Boot with Thymeleaf, and Filestack to support growth for variant access through different hardware mediums.
